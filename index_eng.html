BACK TO SCHOOL

● Introduction ●

Back to School, a tale unwritten.

From preschool to high school, from minor to major, every new semester is a different sentiment. It is the time when resolutions are made, when reveries are being realized. It is the time when friendships are established and relationships are ignited. An ambience of hope and novelty thickens the air. Let us bid our farewells to Winter 2015, and greet the new semester on a high note!

The BackToSchool Party offers, performances, EDM Djs, professional photographers, and UNLIMITED DRINKS, and a myriad more. Let us hold our glasses high, and toast in jubilance as OOC leads the way into Spring 16’!
